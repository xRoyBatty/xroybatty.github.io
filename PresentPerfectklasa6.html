Ćwiczenia z czasu Present Perfect

Zadanie 1: Zadaj koledze/koleżance następujące pytania ze słówkiem "already" i "this week"

Użyj czasowników związanych z gotowaniem lub czasowników nieregularnych ze strony 123 w podręczniku.

  1. Have you already made dinner this week? Yes, I have.
  2. Have you already baked a cake this week? No, I haven't.
  3. Have you already tried a new recipe this week? Yes, I have.
  4. Has your mother already cooked your favorite meal this week? Yes, she has.
  5. Has your brother already burnt something in the kitchen this week? No, he hasn't.

Pamiętaj, aby zapisać odpowiedzi kolegi/koleżanki!

Zadanie 2: Zadaj koledze/koleżance pytania ze zwrotem "Have you ever"

Użyj innych czasowników nieregularnych ze strony 123 niż tych użytych w Zadaniu 1.

  1. Have you ever broken a plate while washing dishes? Yes, I have.
  2. Have you ever forgotten to turn off the stove? No, I never have.
  3. Have you ever eaten something really strange? Yes, I have.
  4. Has your father ever taught you how to cook? Yes, he has.
  5. Has your sister ever won a cooking competition? No, she hasn't.

Pamiętaj, aby zapisać odpowiedzi kolegi/koleżanki!

Zadanie 3: Opisz, co powiedział kolega/koleżanka w Zadaniu 1

Użyj odpowiedzi z Zadania 1, aby napisać zdania o swoim koledze/koleżance.

  1. Ania has already made dinner this week.
  2. Ania hasn't baked a cake this week.
  3. Ania has already tried a new recipe this week.
  4. Ania's mother has already cooked her favorite meal this week.
  5. Ania's brother hasn't burnt anything in the kitchen this week.

Zadanie 4: Opisz, co powiedział kolega/koleżanka w Zadaniu 2

Użyj odpowiedzi z Zadania 2, aby napisać zdania o swoim koledze/koleżance.

  1. Paweł has broken a plate while washing dishes before.
  2. Paweł has never forgotten to turn off the stove.
  3. Paweł has eaten something really strange before.
  4. Paweł's father has taught him how to cook.
  5. Paweł's sister hasn't won a cooking competition.
