Essay 1: Intelligent Houses (Rozprawka)

“Intelligent houses” has been a hot topic recently. These houses have installations and devices that are automatically controlled. Aspects such as comfort, cost and safety are significant to consider if one wants to move into an “intelligent house”. These technologies can make home-life easier as well as more complicated. In my opinion, living in an intelligent house is not a good idea. First and foremost, comfort is what everybody is looking for when moving in. Home is a place where one feels cozy and enjoyable. Simpleness is what people seek in automatically controlled lighting, temperature, air conditioning etc. It seems like a small, not a significant change for household members, but might make their life easier. However, there can be some complications that solutioning would be ten times harder than usual. What if the internet would be lagging? What if the software would be hacked? There are some questions that new technology can not answer due to it being just recently put on the worldwide market. Thus, if one is considering living in an intelligent house, they should consider the unpredictable phenomena that might contribute to their comfortness. On the other hand, intelligent houses are not for low budget people. Big investment might not always equal less spendings in the future. As technology like automatically controlled installations and devices are not so tested and examined as common solutions, due to their recent premiere, can be problematic. Tiny changes in software and the whole installation is working differently. Consequently, it can be a huge financial burden when it comes to repairs. Last but not least is the aspect of safety. Hacking and cyber-robberies happen more often nowadays with rapidly evolving technology. Having a whole house programmed with one software is a great opportunity for cyber-criminals. Safety of household members as well as material goods can be put at risk. Obviously, this is not certain to have a house robbed having intelligent installations, but there is always a slight chance of danger. Summarizing, living in a world where technology is all around us can be misleading. We do not have to have every element of our lives put in software and having artificial intelligence do all the work for us. I think it is better to limit the impact of high-tech on our lives and be still independent on our own.

Essay 3: Road Safety Letter (List do Gazety)

Dear Editors, I’m writing to you with the idea to raise awareness about dangers on the road. Advertisements and campaigns are very often used to point out the importance of safety on the road. Many drivers in recent years have been reckless behind the wheel. Awareness of the possible dangers on the road is a significant aspect of remaining safety during routes. My idea for safe driving is a TV advertisement. It would visualize the tragedy of car accidents that can be caused by reckless drivers. There is many examples of situations that can be used, such as drunk driving, exceeding speed limit, driving without a license, showing off to friends, driving a broken or overused vehicle etc. Each of the situations would contain a perspective of the driver and the victim. Drivers' perspective before the accident should present the behavior or cause of the accident, however the victim's life before the accident should be shown as jubilant, their whole being should be cheerful and seen as important, for example in the surrounding of friends or family. The moment of crash would be tragic and gut wrenching. Post the accident there would be a glance of the victim's family and friends grief, buring their cheerful beloved, or the victim being injured for the rest of their life. Furthermore, there should be a moment for the culprit’s excruciating guilt that would hold in for eternity. The whole advertisement should have accurate exposure, lighting, background music and relevant actors, to express the baggage of emotions holding onto reckless driving. Scientists confirm that fear can robustly deter people from doing something perilous. Fearfulness of committing a life sentence on someone can be successful in encouraging people to drive safely. I hope you will take my suggestion under consideration. With respect, xyz
