Strategic Discussion Questions

  • Given Poland's 27% risk of missing EU targets, should the country prioritize incremental improvements in traditional recycling or pursue comprehensive circular economy transformation?
  • How should Poland balance the €136+ billion EU funding opportunity against documented implementation challenges and fraud risks?
  • Is the Netherlands' 24.5% circularity rate with remaining challenges a realistic benchmark for Poland, or should alternative models be considered?
  • Should Poland's approach prioritize economic efficiency (circular economy) or environmental restoration (regenerative economics) given limited resources?
  • How can Poland address the fundamental tension between consumer price sensitivity (93% prioritize cost) and higher costs of circular products?
  • Given only 22 staff monitor cross-border waste shipments, should investment focus on fraud prevention or system expansion?
  • Would a hybrid approach combining circular economy tools with green economy social measures deliver superior outcomes for Poland?
