Karty do rozmowy - Przykłady odpowiedzi
Poniżej znajdziesz po 3 przykłady dla każdej karty. Użyj ich jako wzór, aby wiedzieć, co możesz powiedzieć na każdy temat.

FAMILY

  • I have a small family - my parents and one sister.
  • My family lives in the same city as me.
  • We usually meet for dinner on Sundays.

NEXT YEAR

  • Next year I want to find a better job.
  • I'm planning to study English more seriously next year.
  • I hope to travel somewhere new next year.

BEST FRIEND

  • My best friend and I studied together at university.
  • I've known my best friend for ten years.
  • My best friend lives in another city now.

JUST ENGLISH?

  • I speak Polish, English, and a little German.
  • English is my second language after Polish.
  • I started learning English when I was twelve.

BAD HABIT

  • I often check my phone too much.
  • I sometimes eat too much chocolate.
  • I have a bad habit of being late for meetings.

WORK EXPERIENCE

  • I worked in a shop during university.
  • My first job was in customer service.
  • I have five years of experience in marketing.

EDUCATION

  • I studied economics at Warsaw University.
  • I finished school in 2015.
  • I'm thinking about doing an MBA course.

WATCHING OR DOING SPORTS

  • I prefer watching football to playing it.
  • I go swimming twice a week.
  • I'm not very good at sports, but I like tennis.

FOOD

  • I love Italian food, especially pasta.
  • I can cook basic meals but nothing special.
  • I try to eat healthy food during the week.

MY WORST HOLIDAY

  • Last year it rained every day during my holiday.
  • I once got food poisoning on holiday in Spain.
  • My worst holiday was when I lost my passport.

DREAM JOB

  • My dream job would be working for a travel company.
  • I'd love to be a photographer for National Geographic.
  • My dream is to have my own small business.

NEXT WEEKEND I'M NOT GOING TO...

  • Next weekend I'm not going to work at all.
  • I'm not going to stay at home - I want to go out.
  • I'm not going to check my emails on Saturday.

I HAVE NEVER...

  • I have never been to Asia.
  • I have never tried sushi.
  • I have never worked in a big corporation.

??? (SURPRISE TOPIC)

  • That's an interesting question!
  • I need a moment to think about that.
  • Can you ask me something else first?

LAST MEETING

  • My last meeting was about our new project.
  • I had a meeting with my boss yesterday.
  • Last week I met with some new clients.

CATS OR DOGS, OR FISH

  • I'm definitely a dog person - cats are too independent.
  • I have a cat because I live in a small apartment.
  • I prefer fish because they're easy to look after.

HOMETOWN

  • I come from a small town near Kraków.
  • My hometown is famous for its old castle.
  • I moved away from my hometown when I was eighteen.

FREE TIME

  • In my free time I like reading books.
  • I usually watch Netflix in the evenings.
  • I enjoy going for walks in the park.

FILMS/BOOKS

  • I prefer action films to romantic comedies.
  • I'm reading a really good book at the moment.
  • I like books more than films because I can use my imagination.

IF I WERE...

  • If I were rich, I would travel around the world.
  • If I were the boss, I would give everyone more holidays.
  • If I were younger, I would study abroad.

PERSON I WON'T FORGET

  • I'll never forget my first English teacher.
  • My grandmother was a very special person.
  • There's a colleague who really helped me when I started work.

Przykłady tworzenia pytań
Te przykłady pokażą Ci, jak tworzyć pytania używając różnych czasów i przyimków. Zwróć uwagę na strukturę każdego pytania.

Present Simple Questions (pytania w czasie teraźniejszym):

  • Do you live with your parents?
  • Do you work on weekends?
  • Do you argue with your colleagues often?
  • Do you worry about your future?
  • Do you agree with your boss's decisions?

Past Simple Questions (pytania w czasie przeszłym):

  • Did you study at university?
  • Did you move to this city last year?
  • Did you talk to your manager yesterday?
  • Did you travel to work by car this morning?
  • Did you listen to music during lunch?

Present Perfect Questions (pytania w czasie present perfect):

  • Have you worked in this company for a long time?
  • Have you lived in this city since 2020?
  • Have you talked to your boss about the promotion?
  • Have you thought about changing jobs?
  • Have you learned from your mistakes at work?

6
Wsparcie do dyskusji - Gotowe zwroty
Dla każdego pytania znajdziesz 3 sposoby rozpoczęcia odpowiedzi i 5 przydatnych słów/wyrażeń.
Pytanie 1: Which of the tips from the video do you find useful? Why?
3 sposoby rozpoczęcia:
  • "I find the tip about... very useful because..."
    Uważam, że rada o... jest bardzo przydatna, ponieważ...
  • "The most helpful tip for me is... because..."
    Najbardziej pomocną radą dla mnie jest... ponieważ...
  • "I think the idea of... works well because..."
    Myślę, że pomysł z... dobrze działa, ponieważ...
5 kluczowych słów/wyrażeń:
  • "practical" - praktyczny
  • "effective" - skuteczny
  • "it makes sense" - to ma sens
  • "reduce stress" - zmniejszyć stres
  • "in my daily life" - w moim codziennym życiu
Pytanie 2: Which of the tips do you consider rather impractical? Why?
3 sposoby rozpoczęcia:
  • "I think the tip about... is not very realistic because..."
    Myślę, że rada o... nie jest bardzo realistyczna, ponieważ...
  • "The idea of... seems difficult to do because..."
    Pomysł z... wydaje się trudny do wykonania, ponieważ...
  • "I don't think... would work in my situation because..."
    Nie sądzę, że... działałoby w mojej sytuacji, ponieważ...
5 kluczowych słów/wyrażeń:
  • "unrealistic" - nierealistyczny
  • "impossible" - niemożliwy
  • "my boss wouldn't allow" - mój szef by nie pozwolił
  • "too expensive" - zbyt drogie
  • "in my workplace" - w moim miejscu pracy
Pytanie 3: Some countries have introduced a 35-hour working week. Do you think it is a good idea? How many hours a week do you have to work in your country?
3 sposoby rozpoczęcia:
  • "I think a 35-hour week is... because in Poland we usually work..."
    Myślę, że 35-godzinny tydzień jest... ponieważ w Polsce zwykle pracujemy...
  • "In my opinion, shorter working hours would be... because..."
    Moim zdaniem krótsze godziny pracy byłyby... ponieważ...
  • "Compared to other countries, Poland has... and I think..."
    W porównaniu z innymi krajami, Polska ma... i myślę...
5 kluczowych słów/wyrażeń:
  • "work-life balance" - równowaga między pracą a życiem
  • "productivity" - produktywność
  • "standard working hours" - standardowe godziny pracy
  • "overtime" - nadgodziny
  • "compared to" - w porównaniu z
Pytanie 4: Can you imagine working only 4 days a week?
3 sposoby rozpoczęcia:
  • "Yes, I can definitely imagine... because..."
    Tak, zdecydowanie mogę sobie wyobrazić... ponieważ...
  • "Actually, working 4 days would be... because..."
    Właściwie, praca 4 dni byłaby... ponieważ...
  • "I'm not sure if 4 days would work because..."
    Nie jestem pewien, czy 4 dni by działało, ponieważ...
5 kluczowych słów/wyrażeń:
  • "three-day weekend" - trzydniowy weekend
  • "more time for" - więcej czasu na
  • "financial concerns" - obawy finansowe
  • "less money" - mniej pieniędzy
  • "it depends on" - to zależy od
Pytanie 5: What are your own rules that you follow to reduce stress and feel good at work?
3 sposoby rozpoczęcia:
  • "My personal rule is to... because it helps me..."
    Moją osobistą zasadą jest... ponieważ to mi pomaga...
  • "I always try to... and I never... because..."
    Zawsze staram się... i nigdy nie... ponieważ...
  • "One thing that really works for me is..."
    Jedna rzecz, która naprawdę u mnie działa, to...
5 kluczowych słów/wyrażeń:
  • "personal strategy" - osobista strategia
  • "set boundaries" - wyznaczyć granice
  • "take regular breaks" - robić regularne przerwy
  • "switch off" - odłączyć się
  • "works for me" - działa u mnie
